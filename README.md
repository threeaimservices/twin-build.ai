# Twin-Build.AI

Digital Twins for the built Environment.

We offer strategic and implementation services for asset owner operators wanting to build their own Digital Twins without replacing existing systems.

The service is based on a platform independant data catalog from our AI partners Xerini which connects and aligns data from engineering and operational platforms.The catalog maintains data lineage, tracks data quality and harnesses AI to do the hard stuff like generating and evolving target data models, setting up and maintaining connections and orchestrating data pipelines to convert siloed data of varied quality so it becomes meanignful, trusted and re usable. 

The service is an independant layer and owned by none of the platforms it connects  - as it should be. 

The services include: 

1) Handover digital twin  - A service for EPCs and Contractor to support Data readiness and the progressive handover of data to Asset Owner operators  

2) Operational digital twin - A service to conenct engineering and operational data to provide asset owner operators with a living digital twin. 

3) Data Assurance Gateway  - A Service for EPCs / Contractors and asset owners to automatically verify data against information requirements

4) Domestic digital twin  - A  white label service where home owners upload drawings and meter connections.
